Pro Advice

Quick Design Tips

Bite-sized insights from our design team to elevate your space — no consultation required.

01
Start with a Focal Point
Every room needs one hero piece — a bold sofa, a statement light, or an oversized art piece. Build your palette around it.
02
Layer Your Lighting
Combine ambient, task, and accent lighting at three different heights to create depth and warmth in any room.
03
Mix High & Low Textures
Pair smooth surfaces (glass, marble) with tactile ones (linen, wool, rattan) for a space that feels curated, not flat.
04
Scale Matters
Oversized art in a small room creates drama; petite furniture in a large room feels lost. Measure twice, buy once.
