Our Journey

Where It All Began

Lumière Home was founded in 2009 by interior designer Elena Beaumont in a small London workshop. What began as a bespoke furniture atelier has grown into an internationally recognised luxury brand with showrooms across three continents.

Our philosophy has always been simple: create furniture that doesn't just fill a room, but transforms it. Every piece we craft tells a story — of the forest it came from, the hands that shaped it, and the life it will become part of.

Today, Lumière Home employs over 200 craftspeople, designers, and interior consultants, united by a shared passion for extraordinary furniture and exceptional living spaces.

Our Mission

Committed to a Greener Future

At Lumière Home, sustainability is not an afterthought — it's woven into every decision we make, from material sourcing to final delivery. We believe luxury and responsibility can coexist beautifully.

Our 2030 Sustainability Pledge commits us to net-zero carbon operations, 100% renewable energy in our workshops, and a closed-loop manufacturing process that creates zero waste.
